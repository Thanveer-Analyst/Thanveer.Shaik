Research Experience

Research Project 1: Remote Patient Monitoring via Non-invasive Digital Technology using Artificial Intelligence

From 2020 to Present, I have been working on adapting AI methodologies in monitoring hospitalized patients’ suicidal tendencies in an acute mental health facility. Using non-invasive digital technology, near-field coherent sensing (NCS) allows patients to perform daily tasks without direct skin contact. Successful implementation of this project would enhance patient care and assist medical staff and doctors in hospitals.

Publications:

  • Shaik et al. QXAI: Explainable AI Framework for Quantitative Analysis in Patient Monitoring Systems. Submitted to Pattern Recognition (March, 2023). [Under Review]

Research Project 2: Educational Decision Support System Adopting Sentiment Analysis on Student Feedback

From 2021 to Present, I have been focusing on analyzing teaching practice and learning environments by processing unstructured student feedback and forum posts. This project aims to design and develop a decision-making support system to improve the operational effectiveness of teaching and learning across the university.

Publications:

  • Shaik et al. Educational Decision Support System Adopting Sentiment Analysis on Student Feedback. In Proceedings of the 21st IEEE/WIC/ACM International Conference on Web Intelligence Agent Technology 2022 Nov 27.
